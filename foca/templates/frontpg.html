About Catering


Tradition since 2017

Food Catering & Service is a event managing & wedding catering company that is based in Kolkata. Having more than 3 years of experience in the industry they have well-trained master chefs who cook delicious cuisines for their clients and guests. Presenting their dishes in the most unique and creative manner, Food Catering & Service is a one-stop shop for getting sumptuous cuisines for your pre-wedding and post-wedding functions.

We are one of the very few “Catering Management Group in Kolkata” who serve their guests with international palettes by professional management team from the hospitality industry.Cuisines & Capacity: Food Catering & Service offers their catering services to minimum 100 people and maximum 5000 people at the events. Greeting their guests graciously they have Veg and Non Veg options available, which are made with much love and affection. They offer the following cuisines: Indian Chinese Italian Bengali Thai Desserts.
